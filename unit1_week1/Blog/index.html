My three favorite websites are : pinterest, okayplayer, and buzzfeed

Pinterest: The name of the site describes its’ purpose best: it pins your interests. This is one of the sites I live on the web. I love the concept of the site—finding ideas and inspiration from everything from cooking to fashion. The interface is easy and intuitive to navigate. I have a lot of fun on this site and can spend wayyy too much time discovering new things here.

    Visuals:

  1. Colorful
  2. Crowded
  3. Busy
  4. Abundant

    Content:

  1. Inviting
  2. Playful
  3. Engaging
  4. Curious

Purpose:

I would say that this site accomplishes it’s goal of becoming a tool for discovering ideas from the web and having a place to store them and find new inspiration. Pinterest’s co-founder Ben Silbermann says that he created the site, “just picking up on something people were already doing and making it easier for them to do it.”
